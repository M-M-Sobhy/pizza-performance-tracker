# 🍕 Pizza Sales Performance Dashboard

A dynamic and interactive Excel Data Dashboard designed to analyze pizza sales performance, identify consumer behavioral patterns, and uncover actionable business insights from raw transactional data.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview
<img width="1765" height="513" alt="Screenshot 2026-06-02 220148" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/58d48737-b21a-4daa-aef7-341af1b4850b" />


The **Pizza Sales Dashboard** provides a centralized, visual representation of key operational and commercial metrics. It translates raw order details into visual trends, enabling stakeholders to evaluate sales efficiency, volume metrics, and categorical popularity at a glance.

### Key Insights & Metrics Captured:
* **Core Business KPIs:** Tracks overall financial and operational health via Total Sales (**$8,436**), Total Orders (**499**), and Total Volume/Quantity Sold (**511 items**).
* **Categorical Performance:** Visual breakdown of orders by pizza category (**Classic** leads with 149 orders, followed closely by **Veggie** and **Supreme**).
* **Dimensional Preferences:** Size-based sales analysis showing that **Large (L)** pizzas dominate consumer preference at **47%** of total volume, while **Extra Large (XL)** represents a niche market at **3%**.
* **Temporal Trends:** * **Hourly Activity:** An interactive trendline tracking peak order hours, highlighting a minor lunch spike at **12 PM** and a major dinner surge peaking at **5 PM** (72 orders).
    * **Monthly Seasonality:** A volume chart showing customer engagement across May, June, July, August, and September, with **June** peaking as the most active month (54 orders).
* **Granular Filtering:** Integrated multi-slicer controls allowing deep-dives by **Order Date (Timeline by Quarters)**, **Pizza Category**, and **Pizza Size**.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Features

* **Platform:** Microsoft Excel
* **Data Processing:** Power Query (Cleaned, transformed, and modeled raw transactional records).
* **Analysis Engine:** Pivot Tables (Aggregated data across temporal, categorical, and physical dimensions).
* **UI/UX Design:** Custom visual theme featuring a clean layout, consistent typographic hierarchy, and descriptive iconography.
* **Interactivity:** Cross-filtering dynamic Pivot Charts via connected Slicers and an interactive Timeline tracker.

---

## 📈 Key Findings
1. **The Dinner Surge:** Operational capacity and staffing should be optimized around **4:00 PM – 6:00 PM**, where order volume experiences its highest daily velocity.
2. **Product Optimization:** "The Classic Deluxe Pizza" and "The Pepperoni Pizza" are the top volume drivers. Inventory management should prioritize ingredients for these high-turnover items.
3. **Size Differentiation:** Large and Medium sizes constitute **74%** of total consumer demand. Marketing campaigns and promotional combos should heavily leverage these form factors.

## 🚀 How to Use the Dashboard
1. Download or clone this repository to your local machine.
2. Open the `.xlsx` file using **Microsoft Excel 2016 or newer** (for full Slicer and Timeline compatibility).
3. Use the **Filters / Slicers panel** on the right side to filter the entire dashboard by specific dates, categories, or sizes.
4. Clear filters using the clear-filter icon on the top-right of each slicer box to return to the global view.
